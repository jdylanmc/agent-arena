# Bot Fight (historical alias)

Original codename for what is now **Agent Arena**. Used in early
prototyping (`prototype/swarm-primary.png` still shows the *Bot Fight*
brand in the top-left). Renamed during `/speckit.specify` for the
scaffold spec because the prior `llm-bot-fight` repo (a separate
prototype) was diverging from the orchestration-engine vision and the
new repo wanted a less combative framing.

**Use Agent Arena going forward.** The `Bot Fight` term lives only as a
historical artifact and shows up in:

- `prototype/swarm-*.png` — the visual prototype's branding header. Per
  CD-08, the prototype's *layout* is the binding visual contract; the
  *brand text* is updated to "Agent Arena" in the implementation.
- This glossary entry.
